How ScrollScholar works

ScrollScholar is designed to help students redirect moments of distraction into moments of recall and review.
1

Add your study notes

Paste lecture notes, scan study pages, or import summaries. ScrollScholar uses your own material as the basis for learning challenges.

2

Generate AI flashcards

The app creates concise study questions from your notes so the challenges feel personalized, relevant, and actually useful.

3

Unlock with a brain challenge

During study time, selected distracting apps can require a quick challenge before access is granted, helping turn distraction into review.

Designed for real students

ScrollScholar combines AI flashcards, study mode, and focus mechanics in one streamlined experience.

Instead of relying on generic questions, ScrollScholar uses the user’s own notes as the basis for AI-generated flashcards. The result is a more personal and useful study flow, especially for quiz prep, recall practice, and daily review.

Privacy and user control

ScrollScholar is designed around user choice and limited use of device-control features.

User-selected apps only

ScrollScholar only applies restrictions to apps explicitly selected by the user.

No monitoring of personal content

ScrollScholar does not read messages, browsing content, or activity inside other apps.

Study notes stay tied to learning

Notes added by the user are used to generate flashcards and learning challenges inside the app.
