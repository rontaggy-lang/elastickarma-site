ScrollScholar

ScrollScholar helps students turn moments of distraction into moments of learning.

Users add study notes and ScrollScholar generates AI flashcards. When a user tries to open distracting apps during study time, a quick flashcard challenge can appear before the app unlocks.

Features

• AI flashcards from your notes
• Focus challenges before unlocking apps
• Study mode for quick review
• Scroll IQ progress tracking
