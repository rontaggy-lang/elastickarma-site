About ElasticKarma

ElasticKarma Labs is an indie app development lab focused on designing simple, thoughtful mobile tools that help people learn better, focus deeper, and build healthier relationships with technology.

We build lightweight iOS apps that combine modern AI capabilities with clean design and strong privacy principles.

Our philosophy is simple: software should help people think, learn, and grow—not just capture their attention.

Our Mission

Modern technology is optimized for distraction. ElasticKarma Labs explores the opposite idea: software that strengthens attention and learning.

We experiment with new ways to combine AI, behavioral design, and simple interfaces to help users stay focused while still benefiting from modern mobile technology.

Our Apps

ScrollScholar

ScrollScholar helps students reduce social media distractions while studying. Users can create flashcards from their own notes and optionally require a quick learning challenge before unlocking selected distracting apps.
