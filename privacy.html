Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how ElasticKarma Labs and its apps, including ScrollScholar and Hydration Lock, handle user information.

Information users provide

Some ElasticKarma apps may store user-generated content such as study notes, preferences, or habit tracking data that users voluntarily provide inside the app.

How information is used

Information provided by users is used only to support the functionality of the app. For example:

  • ScrollScholar uses study notes to generate learning challenges and flashcards.
  • Hydration Lock records hydration progress and streak information to help users build healthy habits.

Use of Screen Time / Family Controls APIs

Some ElasticKarma apps may use Apple's Screen Time / Family Controls APIs. These APIs allow users to voluntarily restrict access to apps they select.

ElasticKarma apps do not read messages, browsing history, or content from other apps.

Data sharing

ElasticKarma Labs does not sell or share personal data with third parties.
