Privacy Policy

ScrollScholar respects your privacy.

The app stores study notes provided by the user in order to generate flashcards and learning challenges. These notes are used only for the learning features of the app.

ScrollScholar may use Apple's Screen Time APIs to apply restrictions to apps explicitly selected by the user. The app does not monitor messages, browsing history, or activity within other apps.

ScrollScholar does not sell personal data.
