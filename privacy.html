Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how ElasticKarma Labs and its app ScrollScholar handle user information.

Information users provide

ScrollScholar may store notes, study material, or text that users voluntarily add in order to generate flashcards and learning challenges.

How study notes are used

Study notes are used to generate AI-powered flashcards and learning challenges inside the app. These notes are used to support the app’s study and focus features.

Use of Screen Time / Family Controls APIs

ScrollScholar may use Apple’s Screen Time / Family Controls APIs to apply restrictions to apps explicitly selected by the user during focus or study periods.

ScrollScholar does not read messages, browsing history, or content inside other apps.

Data sharing

ElasticKarma Labs does not sell personal data.
