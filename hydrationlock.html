Hydration Lock

Hydration Lock helps users build healthier habits by encouraging them to drink water before opening distracting apps.

When a locked app is opened, Hydration Lock prompts the user to complete a simple hydration challenge. Once completed, the app unlocks temporarily so the user can continue.

How It Works

1. Select Apps to Lock

Choose which apps you want to temporarily restrict using the system app picker.

2. Drink Water

When you try to open a locked app, Hydration Lock prompts you to drink water first.

3. Unlock and Continue

After completing the hydration challenge, your apps unlock briefly so you can continue using them.

Why Hydration Matters

Staying hydrated improves focus, energy levels, and overall wellbeing. Hydration Lock helps turn this healthy behavior into a simple daily habit.
