How it works

Hydration Lock adds light, intentional friction before selected apps open, making it easier to reinforce a healthy habit throughout the day.
1

Select your apps

Choose which distracting apps you want to place behind a hydration challenge using the system app picker.

2

Complete the challenge

When you try to open a locked app, Hydration Lock prompts you to drink water and complete a quick challenge flow.

3

Unlock and continue

After the challenge is completed, your selected apps unlock briefly so you can continue with a healthier habit already reinforced.

Designed to make healthy behavior easier to repeat

Hydration Lock is intentionally simple. The goal is not to create a heavy wellness app, but a lightweight system users can actually live with every day.

The experience combines a short unlock challenge, streaks, XP, milestone progression, and visual rewards so hydration feels more engaging instead of just another reminder to ignore.

Privacy and user control

Hydration Lock is designed around user choice and a limited, transparent use of Apple’s device-control features.

User-selected apps only

Hydration Lock only applies restrictions to apps explicitly selected by the user.

No monitoring of personal content

Hydration Lock does not read messages, browsing content, or activity inside other apps.

Habit data stays tied to the product experience

Hydration progress, streaks, and milestone data are used to support the app’s habit-building features inside the app.
